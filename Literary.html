At Criticspace, we offer Magazine Features, Author Spotlight, Interviews, Book Reviews, Editorial Features, and Anthologies through The Literature Today.

The Literature Today is a literary magazine that showcases authors, books, publishers, educators, entrepreneurs, and creative professionals from diverse backgrounds. Published in multiple editions throughout the year, the magazine features author interviews, book reviews, literary discussions, editorial articles, and inspiring stories from the literary world.

The magazine is available through leading digital reading platforms, helping featured content reach readers across different regions and communities.
