Book Marketing Services in India

Helping Books Reach the Right Readers

Publishing a book is only the first step. The challenge begins when authors try to reach readers in a market where thousands of new books are released every day. Think about movies, OTT series, smartphones, or everyday products. Before people buy them, they first hear about them through marketing. Books work the same way. No matter how good a story is, readers cannot discover it if they do not know it exists.

Book Consultation Contact Us

At Criticspace, we help authors increase visibility through strategic book marketing designed to connect books with the right audience. Our services include Book Launch Campaigns, Amazon Book Promotion, Goodreads Promotion, Book Publicity Services, Book Sales Strategy, and Reader Engagement Campaigns. Each activity is planned to improve discoverability, create awareness, and encourage meaningful interaction between authors and readers.

Rather than relying on a single promotional method, successful book marketing combines publicity, audience engagement, digital visibility, and long-term positioning. The goal is not simply promotion—it is helping books gain attention in a competitive literary landscape.

Whether you are a debut writer, self-published author, or traditionally published creator, effective marketing can help your work reach more readers and build lasting recognition.
Our Solutions

Our Book Marketing Services

🚀

Book Launch Campaigns

A well-planned book launch creates momentum and visibility at the most important stage of a book's journey. Launch campaigns are designed to generate awareness, reader interest, and early engagement around a new release.

🎯

Amazon Book Promotion

Amazon is one of the largest book discovery platforms in the world. Strategic promotion on Amazon helps improve visibility, discoverability, and reader access for both new and existing titles.

⭐

Goodreads Promotion

Goodreads connects books with active readers, reviewers, and literary communities interested in discovering new titles. A focused Goodreads presence helps authors build credibility within engaged reading audiences.

📰

Book Publicity Services

Publicity helps books gain attention through reviews, media mentions, interviews, and editorial coverage that reach readers across different platforms and communities.

📈

Book Sales Strategy

A thoughtful sales strategy looks at positioning, pricing, platform selection, and audience targeting to help books perform better in the market over time.

💬

Reader Engagement Campaigns

Building a connection with readers goes beyond a single purchase. Engagement campaigns focus on creating ongoing interest, encouraging reviews, and developing a loyal reader community around an author's work.
