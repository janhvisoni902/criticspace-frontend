Refund & Cancellation Policy

Due to the nature of digital, marketing, branding, PR, content creation, and promotional services, payments made for completed or ongoing work are generally non-refundable.

Project Cancellation Before Commencement

Clients may request project cancellation before work has commenced. Any approved refund will be processed after deducting applicable administrative, consultation, planning, or processing charges.

Execution & Ongoing Services

Once project execution, content creation, media outreach, promotional activities, website development, or any agreed service has started, refunds may not be available for the completed portion of the work.

Partial Cancellations

If a project is cancelled by the client after partial completion, payment for the completed work and resources utilized shall remain payable.

Case-by-Case Review

Any refund request will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis and the final decision shall remain at the discretion of Criticspace.

Need More Information?

If you have questions regarding our policies or services, our team will be happy to assist you.
