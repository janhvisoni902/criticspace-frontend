About Criticspace

Book Marketing, Author Branding & Media Visibility

Founded in 2016, Criticspace has grown into one of India's leading literary marketing and author branding platforms. Over the years, we have worked with 5000+ authors and books across multiple genres, supporting both traditionally published and self-published writers through strategic promotion, branding, publicity, and reader engagement campaigns.

Criticspace has collaborated with books and authors associated with renowned publishing houses including HarperCollins India, Rupa Publications, Jaico Publishing House, Garuda Prakashan, White Falcon Publishing, and many more.

Our goal is simple: To help books reach the right readers and help authors build long-term literary recognition.
