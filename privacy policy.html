Privacy Policy

At Criticspace, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Company or Organization Name
  • Book or Project Details
  • Information submitted through contact forms, emails, or inquiries

We may also collect basic website usage data, including browser type, device information, and pages visited.

How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Provide requested services
  • Share proposals, quotations, and project updates
  • Improve our website and services
  • Maintain business records

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Client Content

Authors, publishers, businesses, and professionals may submit books, manuscripts, images, biographies, articles, or other materials for promotional purposes.

By submitting content, you confirm that you have the necessary rights and permissions to share such material with Criticspace.

Third-Party Platforms

Some services may involve third-party platforms such as social media websites, news portals, search engines, or digital publications. Criticspace is not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of these external platforms.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and analyze website traffic. Users may disable cookies through their browser settings if preferred.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information shared with us. However, no online system can guarantee complete security.

Changes to This Policy

Criticspace may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
